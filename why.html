Wondering what Snapchat is? Well it's an app where you can take photos and use filters to create good memories. The first photo shows the basic layout of Snapchat. The 2nd photo shows the filters you have access to as a user. These types of filters animate things on to your face. You can have dog ears, hearts floating above your head or glasses with a cool background!

There are more filters on Snapchat after you take a photo. There are the filters that change the color of the photo and give it a specific theme. Then there are filters that have text and cool designs as shown in the 2nd photo. They are mainly used for big events or holidays.

You also have stickers in Snapchat!! After taking a picture, you can create stickers of people by cropping the face you want to use. 1st photo is an example. The 2nd photo shows stickers with text on it and cool designs. You can add them anywhere on your photo. They also have GIFs that can make pictures funnier.

These following pictures show that you can add text or a bitmoji in your picture. You can use different types of fonts to add caption to your photos. Also you can create a bitmoji of yourself that have different expressions to use as a way to make a photo funnier and more understanding.
